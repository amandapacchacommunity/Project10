Analytical Notes

  • Large departments may not always be the highest risk, but they often deserve monitoring due to operational dependency.
  • Vacancy concentration can indicate service disruption, hiring bottlenecks, or budget-planning pressure.
  • Use both absolute counts and relative ratios to avoid overstating risk in large departments.

Risk Interpretation

Workforce concentration and vacancy clustering are useful early warning indicators. Even without complex forecasting, they help frame where operational resilience may be weaker and where staffing conditions could affect delivery capacity.
