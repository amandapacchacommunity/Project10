Suggested Interpretation Rules

  • Use budget books, recovery plans, and departmental materials as evidence layers, not perfect fact tables.
  • Tag records or departments using transparent logic and document manual assumptions.
  • Separate direct source facts from inferred restructuring indicators.
  • Frame findings as structural signals, not claims of complete organizational design.

Risk Interpretation

Rapid structural growth can introduce onboarding strain, documentation gaps, classification drift, and long-term sustainability questions, especially when new staffing patterns appear linked to temporary funding or emergency response.
