Suggested Interpretation Standard

  • Treat 2022 materials as structural evidence, not a complete organization map.
  • Document every inferred flag or department signal clearly.
  • Separate direct public facts from analytical interpretation.
  • Use cautious wording such as “suggests,” “indicates,” or “signals.”

Limitation

This page reflects public structural signals only. It does not reconstruct full internal reporting lines, complete org charts, or official internal reorganization decisions.
