Compensation Comparison

Use standardized role groups rather than raw titles for pay comparison. This makes compensation review more realistic and reduces the risk of comparing titles that are similar in wording but inconsistent in grouping.

Direct Source Fields Used Here

  • Published position title
  • Department
  • Salary or hourly rate fields
  • Classification and pay-plan reference materials where applicable

Derived Fields Used Here

  • Standardized title group
  • Mapping confidence
  • Classification consistency flag
  • Compensation variance indicator

Suggested Mapping Standard

  • Normalize capitalization, punctuation, abbreviations, and obvious duplicates.
  • Map raw titles to standardized groups only when the logic is documented.
  • Retain an “unmapped” or “manual review” category where confidence is low.
  • Document uncertain matches rather than forcing false precision.

Limitation

Standardized role groups on this page are derived from public title records and reference materials. They should be read as analytical groupings, not as official internal HR coding.
